Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you serve in Central Florida?

We primarily serve Kissimmee, Orlando, St. Cloud, Lake Nona, and surrounding communities within a 50-mile radius. Long-distance moves are available upon request.

How do you handle delicate or valuable items?

Our team uses specialized packing materials and techniques for valuable items. We offer custom crating services and extra insurance coverage for high-value belongings.

What is your pricing structure?

We offer hourly rates for local moves and flat rates for long-distance relocations. Factors include distance, volume, and specific services needed. All quotes include basic insurance coverage.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking 2-3 weeks in advance for local moves and 4-6 weeks for long-distance moves. Last-minute moves can often be accommodated based on availability.

What insurance options do you provide?

Basic coverage is included with every move. We offer additional full-value protection options and specialized coverage for high-value items. All insurance details are clearly outlined in your moving contract.
